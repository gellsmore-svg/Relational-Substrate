# Concepts

The model is defined in code (`src/model.js`); these docs describe its ideas.

- **[Substrate grammar](substrate-grammar.md)** — route, closure, phase, charge,
  continuity as first-class factors.
- **[Admissibility & outcomes](admissibility-and-outcomes.md)** — open meets closed
  under admissibility → admitted / returned / stored / scattered.
- **[Closure gate & identity](closure-gate-and-identity.md)** — the gate metrics
  and the identity score.
- **[Coherence](coherence.md)** — grammar alignment minus tension and scatter.
- **[Resilience & regimes](resilience-and-regimes.md)** — does identity survive a
  history of encounters, across regimes?
- **[Guardrail](guardrail.md)** — strictly abstract; benches calibrate, they do not
  claim physics.
