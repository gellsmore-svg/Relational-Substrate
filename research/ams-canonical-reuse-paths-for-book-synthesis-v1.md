## Why Reuse Discipline Is Needed

The research corpus is now too large and too internally rich to be reused naively.

Without reuse discipline, book-facing synthesis will tend to fail in one of two ways:

## Failure Mode 1: Overcompression

This happens when the book uses only the very top-level thesis and loses:

- the family grammar;
- the machine bridge;
- the stronger life model;
- the real continuity logic beneath the arc.

That makes the book sound bold but under-argued.

## Failure Mode 2: Research Spill

This happens when the book imports too many:

- scenario files;
- formal side notes;
- local stress tests;
- technical distinctions;

so that the narrative loses shape and the reader loses the main line.

That makes the book feel exhaustive but not well-ordered.

The right path is neither.

It is:

```text
canonical reuse
```
