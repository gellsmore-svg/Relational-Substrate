# AMS Full Ontology Core Thesis Synthesis v5

## Purpose

This note refreshes the full AMS core-thesis synthesis after the ecology layer was canonised and the lower ladder was updated again.

The earlier `v4` thesis already absorbed:

- the stronger machine layer;
- the burden-based life model;
- the refreshed lower ladder;
- the strengthened creation-corruption-redemption arc.

Since then, the corpus has advanced again in one important respect:

- the ecology layer has been canonically mapped;
- the life rung has been restated explicitly as recoverable machine ecology under burden;
- severe-state ecology has been better bounded and integrated.

This file updates the top-level thesis so it reflects that cleaner state rather than trailing it.

## Core Thesis

The current best full-thesis compression is:

```text
Reality is a created bounded continuity whose elemental, machine, living, personal, and redemptive orders are real, layered, and mutually constraining; stable family roles become threshold-bearing runtime machines, runtime machines compose recoverable machine ecologies under real burden, living continuity can be raised into embodied personal subjecthood, corruption parasitically fragments this real creaturely order within continuity, and redemption restores the same damaged creaturely identity into truthful, life-giving, embodied coherence rather than abandoning it, disguising rupture, or replacing the subject altogether.
```

This is now the strongest current full-thesis line in the corpus.

## Why This Is Stronger Than v4

The earlier `v4` thesis already had a stronger machine and life layer than anything before it.

What is stronger now is precision in the middle.

The life rung is no longer best described only as:

- recoverable continuity;
- self-renewing coherence;
- burdened living order.

It is now more exactly:

```text
recoverable machine ecology under burden
```

That matters because it makes the top thesis more explicit about:

- the ecology structure beneath life;
- the role of reserve, support, synchronisation, and cascade;
- the difference between visible activity and owned recoverability;
- the reality of hollow support and mixed spiral death-approach states.

This makes the whole programme more internally coherent.

The strengthened thesis still excludes:

Because created reality is not brute disconnected stuff first.

Because symbolic competence is not enough for:

- redemption.

## 3. Flat Vitalism

Because life is not treated as a mysterious extra without machine or ecology grammar.

## 4. Functionalism

Because visible activity, repair, or output are not identical to:

- living recoverability;

## 5. Discontinuous Dualism

Because embodiment and creaturely continuity remain central.

## 6. Corruption as Rival Principle

Because corruption remains parasitic on what is real.

## 7. Redemption as Replacement or Escape

Because the same damaged identity must remain in view if redemption is to be real.

These exclusions are now more strongly grounded than before.
