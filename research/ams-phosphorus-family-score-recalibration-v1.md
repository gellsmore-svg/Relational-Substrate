## Why Not 10

Do not move phosphorus to 10.

Remaining gaps:

```text
1. Phosphate pathology and mislocated mineral closure need detailed mapping.
2. ATP synthase and chemiosmotic regeneration need dedicated modelling.
3. Concrete signalling pathways such as MAPK, insulin, or cell-cycle phosphorylation need route maps.
4. Phosphate mineral diversity beyond hydroxyapatite is not mapped.
5. Phosphoproteomics and multisite phosphorylation logic are not quantified.
6. RNA catalysis and nucleotide chemistry need deeper treatment.
7. DNA/RNA replication and transcription machinery need enzyme-gate maps.
```

The family is exceptionally strong, but not fully saturated.
