## Issue 3: Limited Front-Matter Native Booking

The current master is clean, but still minimal in book apparatus.

- a Scripture note;
- a note on scope;

The master now implies, correctly, that AI and highly technical formalism sit outside the eight-chapter core.

But if this book is going into active production, that appendix boundary should be made explicit in a later control file.
