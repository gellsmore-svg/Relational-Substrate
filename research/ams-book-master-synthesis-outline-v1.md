# AMS Book Master Synthesis Outline v1

## Purpose

This file defines the master outline for book-facing synthesis drawn from the canonical AMS stack.

The research corpus is now strong enough that the next major gain comes from:

```text
controlled narrative synthesis
```

not from more unconstrained ontology expansion.

The purpose of this file is therefore:

1. to define the main book-facing narrative arc
2. to assign the canonical research inputs to that arc
3. to keep the book structurally strong without becoming a research dump

## Central Claim

The central claim is:

> The book should now be built from a small canonical synthesis stack: the top thesis, the six-family material grammar, the machine bridge, the ecology-backed life synthesis, the machine-life-person ladder, and the strengthened creation-corruption-redemption arc. The narrative should therefore move from created order to living recoverability to personal subjecthood to corruption and redemption, while keeping technical density in reserve rather than foregrounding it indiscriminately.

More briefly:

```text
The book should move in one line from created order to restored creaturely continuity.
```

These are the places where technical density pays the highest narrative return.
